Audio File Playback

Developed by Dr. Andrew Kwok-Fai LUI

Learning Objective: Associate the auditable sound and the audio waveform

The Audio File Playback Application can play AU and WAV audio files. Load the audio file welcome-1.wav. The first 0.5 second has no voice and you can see there are minor fluctuations in the waveform. These fluctuations are noises.

After 0.575 seconds, the voice appeared and you should see that the amplitude of the waveform matches the voice level
