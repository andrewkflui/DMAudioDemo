Wave Harmonics Generator

Developed by Dr. Andrew Kwok-Fai LUI

Learning Objective: Describe the audio effect of adding harmonics to a base frequency

Harmonics give richness and varieties to a musical note. Harmonics is one reason why different instruments sound differently. This learning object offers four harmonics at 1x, 2x, 3x, and 4x the base frequency. Adjust the amplitude of the harmonics and listen to the different audio effect. You should notice that without the harmonics, the signal sounds more dry and simple.
