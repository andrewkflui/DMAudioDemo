Learning Objective: Explain the differences in the perception of various chords

Chords are notes played together. Chords are the basis of musical arrangement and they are used to give sentiment and flow to the melody.

Chords are made up of a base note and other higher notes. Some chords give you a feeling of completeness, and others give you a feeling of suspended. This learning object allows you to experience these feelings.

Select a base note on the left combo box. Then select a chord type from the right combo box. Play the chord. Change to another chord type with the base note unchanged. Play the chord and compare it to the earlier one.
