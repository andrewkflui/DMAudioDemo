Developed by Dr. Andrew Kwok-Fai LUI

Learning Objective: Describe the audio experience of high and low audio frequency signals

The Wave Generator can generate sound of different audio frequencies. Adjust the frequency slider (FREQ) to 200 Hz and listen to the sound. This frequency is around that of human voice. Compare to the sound of the frequency 1200 Hz.

Study the waveform of different frequencies. The amplitude change is quicker if the audio frequency is higher. Change the frequency slider and look at how the waveform changes.
